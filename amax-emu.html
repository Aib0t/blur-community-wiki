Amax Emu

Amax Emu was created as a direct replacement for currently working, but hard to access, Blur online service.

Working features:

  • Profile saving
  • Leaderboards
  • Matchmaking
  • Friends
  • Invites
  • Private games

Upcoming features:

  • Friends challenges
  • Custom game modes
